Common Crop Diseases

  • Northern Leaf Blight (Maize): Apply fungicides (chlorothalonil, mancozeb).
  • Gray Leaf Spot (Maize): Use resistant varieties, apply fungicides.
  • Stalk Rot (Maize): Improve drainage, avoid over-fertilization.
  • Coffee Leaf Rust (Coffee): Apply copper fungicides, prune infected leaves.
  • Coffee Berry Disease (Coffee): Use carbendazim fungicides, maintain field hygiene.
